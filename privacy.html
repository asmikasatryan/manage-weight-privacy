Privacy Policy for Manage Weight

Last updated: 2026-01-01

1. Information we collect

We collect minimal personal data required to provide BMI calculation and tracking: height, weight, age, and optional gender. We may also collect non-personal analytics data such as crash reports and usage statistics.

2. How we use your data

We use your data to calculate BMI, display progress charts, and improve app functionality. Analytics data is used to monitor crashes and improve performance.

3. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share aggregated, non-identifiable analytics with third-party analytics providers (for example Firebase) to improve the app.

4. Data retention and deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data by contacting support at: manage.weight.info@gmail.com. We will delete your data within a reasonable timeframe after verification.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Contact

For questions or data requests, contact: manage.weight.info@gmail.com
